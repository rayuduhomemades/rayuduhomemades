About RAYUDU HOMEMADES

RAYUDU HOMEMADES is a family-run business based in East Godavari, dedicated to creating natural, handmade personal care products. We believe in the power of nature to nurture and heal, which is why all our products are free from harsh chemicals, artificial colors, and synthetic fragrances.

Our journey began with a simple hair powder recipe passed down through generations, and has now grown into a range of products made with the same care and commitment to purity.
